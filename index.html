The machine always follows the order of operations.
Brackets, multiplication and division, addition and subtraction.
Instructions
You need two players.
The game is to reach the goal number first.
Choose a number up to 84. This is the goal.
Players take turns to tap a card to flip it. They say the running total adding the card's value on.
eg player one flips 6 and calls "6" player two flips 5, adds it to the last total and calls "11".
Play continues until the goal is reached or there are no more moves.
The winner is the player who reaches the goal number or the last player to add successfully without going over the goal.
